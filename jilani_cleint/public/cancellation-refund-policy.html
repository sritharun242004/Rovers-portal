Rovers Sports Event Cancellation/Refund Policy

Last Updated: March 25, 2025

Rovers Sports Event Portal ("Rovers") has a strict no-cancellation, no-refunded policy to ensure smooth event planning.

  1. No Cancellations: Once registration is complete and payment is made (INR or AED), cancellations are not permitted. This applies to parents and academies.
  2. No Refunds: All registration fees are non-refundable under any circumstances, including non-attendance, event rescheduling, or participant withdrawal.
  3. Contact: For clarification, email roversgwruae@gmail.com. No refund requests will be entertained post-registration.

By registering, you agree to this policy. Check event details carefully before payment.
