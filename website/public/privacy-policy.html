Privacy Policy

Rovers Sports Event Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 25, 2025

Rovers Sports Event Portal ("Rovers," "we," "us," "our"), operated by Rovers, a company registered in India, is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, store, and safeguard your personal information when you use our website www.rovers.life, register for our events, or interact with our services. By accessing or using our services, you consent to the practices described herein.

1. Scope and Applicability

1.1 This policy applies to all users, including:

  • Parents/guardians registering children.
  • Academies registering students.
  • Volunteers managing checkpoints.
  • Admins overseeing operations. 1.2 It covers personal data collected online and during events (e.g., via QR scans/photos).

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

2.1 Personal Information:

  • Parents: Full name, email address, phone number, payment details.
  • Children/Participants: Full name, date of birth (DOB), gender, ID number (e.g., Aadhaar), ID proof (uploaded), photo (uploaded or captured at checkpoints).
  • Academies: Academy name, contact person’s name, email, phone, address, registration proof.
  • Volunteers: Name, email, phone (assigned by admin).

2.2 Automatically Collected Data:

  • Website Usage: IP address, browser type, device ID, pages visited, timestamps (via cookies/analytics).
  • Event Data: QR code scans (timestamp, checkpoint location), participant photos at checkpoints.

2.3 Payment Information:

  • Processed via payment gateway (card details, transaction ID). We do not store full payment data, only metadata (e.g., payment ID, amount).

2.4 Non-Personal Data:

  • Aggregated statistics (e.g., total registrations) for internal analysis.

3. How We Collect Information

3.1 Directly from You: During account creation, participant registration, or document uploads.

3.2 Automatically: Via website cookies, analytics tools (e.g., Google Analytics), and checkpoint cameras/scanners.

3.3 Third Parties: From payment gatway and event staff (manual entries).

4. Purpose of Collection

We use your information for:

4.1 Event Management:

  • Process registrations and payments.
  • Generate QR codes for bibs.
  • Track participant progress (arrival, sports entry, exit).

4.2 Safety and Verification:

  • Verify identities via ID proofs and photos.
  • Ensure all participants are accounted for during the event.

4.3 Communication:

  • Send registration confirmations, event updates, and QR codes via email/SMS.
  • Respond to support queries.

4.4 Improvement:

  • Analyze usage data to enhance our website and events.
  • Compile anonymized reports (e.g., participation stats).

4.5 Legal Compliance:

  • Fulfill obligations under Indian law (e.g., tax records, child safety).

5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

5.1 With Service Providers:

  • Payment Gateway: For payment processing (INR/AED).
  • Hosting Providers: [AWS/Mongo DB] for data storage.
  • Communication Tools: Email/SMS providers (e.g., SES).

5.2 With Event Staff:

  • Volunteers/Admins: Access participant names, QR codes, and photos for checkpoint operations.

5.3 Legal Requirements:

  • We may disclose data if required by law, court order, or government authority

5.4 No Sale: We do not sell or rent your personal data to third parties for marketing.

6. Data Storage and Security

6.1 Location: Data is stored on secure servers in AWS/Mongo DB..

6.2 Security Measures:

  • Encryption: HTTPS for website, AES-256 for stored data.
  • Access Controls: Role-based access (e.g., volunteers see limited data).
  • Monitoring: Regular audits to detect breaches.

6.3 Risk: While we strive to protect your data, no system is 100% secure. We are not liable for unauthorized access beyond our control.

7. Your Rights (Under DPDP Act, 2023)

7.1 Access: Request a copy of your data (email [support@roverssports.com]).

7.2 Correction: Ask to update inaccurate information.

7.3 Deletion: Request removal of non-essential data (e.g., photos post-event), subject to legal retention needs.

7.4 Withdraw Consent: Stop future data collection by closing your account (doesn’t affect prior processing).

7.5 Process: Submit requests in writing; we’ll respond within 30 days.

8. Cookies and Tracking

8.1 Purpose: Cookies track login sessions, preferences, and site usage.

8.2 Types:

  • Essential: Enable core functionality (e.g., login).
  • Analytics: Measure traffic (e.g., Google Analytics).

8.3 Control: Disable non-essential cookies via browser settings, though this may limit site features.

9. Children’s Privacy

9.1 Age: Participants are 5–21; data is collected with parental consent via the portal.

9.2 Protection: We limit child data use to event purposes only.

9.3 Guardians: Parents/academies are responsible for consenting on behalf of minors.

10. International Users (e.g., AED Payers)

10.1 Cross-Border: Data from UAE users (paying in AED) is processed in India per Indian law.

10.2 Compliance: We aim to align with data protection standards

11.2 Updates will be posted on [www.roverssports.com/privacy] and, if significant, notified via email. Continued use after changes implies consent.
